Design patterns

Here you will find the latest CJS CPP Design Patterns for designers. These patterns can be individual 'elements' or 'components' (a collection of 'elements' used together).

This is an addendum to the GDS Design Patterns and GOV.UK elements as well as informing the Single Page Platform Development Kit (PDK) to help designers to create content and prototypes for the CJS Common Platform.
