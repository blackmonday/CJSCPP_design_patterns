Global notification

Last updated on 20 February 2018

Description

This pattern is a message displayed at the top of the page above the header and is typically used when there are technical or connectivity issues that affect the platform and/or user globally (not case specific).

Your session has expired due to a period of inactivity

With alert icon.

Your session has expired due to a period of inactivity

With no icon.

Your session has expired due to a period of inactivity - sign in

With text link - the entire banner is clickable

Example showing placement above the global header

Content only spans the content width as shown. The red background spans the full width in the same way as the header black background with no padding.
