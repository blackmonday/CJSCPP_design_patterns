Grids

Description

We use the GOV.UK standard grid unit proportions.

Examples

Halves

The content area is divided into two equal width columns.

Thirds

The content are is divided into three equal width columns.

Two thirds / one third

This divides the content area into a large left column and a smaller right column.

One third / two thirds

This divides the content area into a small left column and a larger right column.

Quarters

This divides the content area into four equal width columns.

Additional information

The default maximum page width is 1020px, but go wider if the content requires it.

Gutters are 15px for smaller screens and 30px for larger screens.
