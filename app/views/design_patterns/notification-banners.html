Notification banners

Description

This pattern can be used when there is important information to tell the user.

Examples

Turqoise banner

Typically used for confirmation and non-critical notifications.

Green banner

Typically used to to highlight active status.

Orange banner

Typically used to to highlight pending status/actions.

Red banner

Typically used to show locked status or when an action is unresolved.

Grey banner

Typically used to show degregistered/inactive status or to display important numbers.

Redline specification

Typically used to show degregistered/inactive status or to display important numbers.
