Notification banners

Last updated on 27 February 2018

Description

This pattern can be used when there is important information to tell the user. There are different colours available to offer greater flexibility based on the importance of the information being conveyed.

.notification-banner {
    color: #FFFFFF;
    font-size: 24px;
    font-weight: bold;
