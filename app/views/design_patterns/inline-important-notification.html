Inline important notification

Last updated on 20 February 2018

Description

We need to convey the gravity of the situation weâ€™re describing. In some instances the important notification exists to tell users that if they continue, some or all of their work will be irretrievably lost.

We also need to convey important information for the user to consider, however, they can continue without loosing any of their work.

We need to ensure colour blind users will be able to interprest these importan notifications. The use of an icon or indentation will aid with this. As will the interaction - for examle the important notifications are display only when a user take a specific action so there will be a noticable shift of content on the screen.

Concept

When a change has implications a short message with a tickbox can be used for users to confirm the change before saving.
